About Zuari Infinity & Trading LLC

Zuari Infinity & Trading LLC was founded in Virgina, USA, with the ambition of building a truly global footprint in investment and trading. From energy to real estate to international commodities, our strength lies in connecting opportunities across regions and industries. With established credit lines in India, the Middle East, Europe, and Singapore, we are actively bridging global capital with US-based ventures, creating a seamless flow of growth and innovation.

Our strategy is built on trust, long-term partnerships, and financial strength. By linking global credit lines to our US operations, we are building a structure that ensures agility, resilience, and scalability. This approach positions us to deliver value not only to businesses we partner with but also to the wider economies where we operate.

As we continue expanding, our focus remains on fostering meaningful relationships with stakeholders and driving business outcomes that are sustainable, forward-looking, and globally relevant.
